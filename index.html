CrystalsCollector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if you go above that number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click it.

Each time when the game starts, the new game will change the values of the crystals.
